Melek Rzazade

Melek Rzazade is a Computer Science student with a minor in Cognitive Psychology at Constructor University Bremen, where she focuses on Human–Computer Interaction, Social Robotics, and Computer Vision. Her work explores how intelligent systems can better understand human behavior and support meaningful interaction.

Beyond academics, she actively contributes to campus life. She works as a Data Analyst in Alumni & Career Services and serves as a Residential Support Assistant, supporting student wellbeing and community connection. As the Google Developer Groups (GDG) Campus Lead and Computer Science Major Representative, she organizes workshops, technical sessions, and initiatives that strengthen the university’s CS community.
